About the Project

This project utilizes Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to detect potholes in road images. It is trained on a dataset of normal and pothole images to help in road maintenance and safety.

  • Technology Stack: Python, Machine Learning
  • Preprocessing: Image normalization, resizing, and grayscale conversion
  • Goal: Assist authorities in identifying road hazards efficiently
